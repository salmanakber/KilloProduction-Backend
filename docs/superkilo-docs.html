Platform Overview

SuperKillo is a unified super-platform where commerce, mobility, service providers, and finance operate on one backend. Customers can buy products, request services, book riders, transfer money, and track every lifecycle from request to settlement — while administrators control KYC, security, operations, and policy.

Core Pillars
  • Commerce: Pharmacy, Food, Grocery, Auto Parts, Supplier sourcing
  • Services: Mechanic quotes, offers, job execution, and approval flow
  • Mobility: Ride booking, courier dispatch, rider acceptance, live tracking
  • Finance: Wallet, transfers, withdrawals, gateway processing, refunds
  • Admin: KYC, compliance, commissions, security, reporting, HR
Shared Infrastructure
  • AI layer for recommendation, virtual assistant, and intelligent drafting
  • Socket layer for dispatch, chat, status updates, and presence
  • Order lifecycle engine for split orders, pickup, delivery, completion
  • Commission & tax handling with module-specific payout calculations
  • Role-based access model for admin features and operational safety
  • Notification infrastructure: in-app, email, SMS, and mobile push
Messaging architecture note: Marketing campaigns drive growth and conversions; system notices communicate operational truth (incidents, maintenance, policy); both coexist with transactional messages (orders, payouts, OTPs) delivered through the same technical channels where appropriate.
Commerce
01
Pharmacy Module

Built as both a healthcare-assist module and a medicine commerce engine — linking AI symptom support, prescription processing, pharmacy matching, chat/approval workflow, checkout, rider logistics, and health continuity records.

Customer Experience
  • Virtual doctor flow: describe symptoms via text, voice, or image and receive medically guided suggestions.
  • Prescription upload: prescriptions are analyzed and transformed into structured medicine suggestions.
  • Nearby pharmacy intelligence: location-aware listing with stock/availability and delivery checks.
  • Pharmacy chat handoff: continue clarification in chat when AI suggests uncertain items.
  • Purchase flow: address, payment, and confirmation handled in-app.
Backend / Operations
  • Single-store first: attempts complete fulfillment from one pharmacy before split sourcing.
  • Prescription queue: pharmacies receive queued requests with status control.
  • Top-selling & search endpoints: powers medicine discovery and fast purchase conversion.
  • Payment/settlement hooks: provisional vendor crediting and final settlement on completion.
  • Refund integration: supports post-order refund handling including reverse pickup logistics.
Health Integration
  • Medicine reminders & vitals connect with ongoing health management data.
  • Doctor visit and lab context support better medication continuity decisions.
Workflow
Symptom / prescription input→ AI extraction & medicine shortlist→ Pharmacy review + chat→ Customer payment→ Rider pickup & delivery→ Completion settlement
Commerce
02
Supplier (Wholesaler) Module

Enables pharmacies/vendors to source medicines and products from wholesalers with quote negotiation, courier-enabled delivery, payment/commission handling, and inventory import after delivery.

Supplier Lifecycle
  • Wholesaler onboarding & KYC: create, verify, approve, suspend, and govern wholesaler accounts.
  • Catalog management: wholesalers maintain products and medicine lists for B2B sourcing.
  • Supplier discovery: pharmacies can browse wholesalers and evaluate before ordering.
Quote & Order
  • Quote request creation with needed items and terms.
  • Supplier response options: accept, reject, or counter with changed conditions.
  • Terms confirmation before acceptance and payment progression.
  • Delivery coordination with courier booking for wholesaler-to-pharmacy movement.
  • QR-secured handover for controlled pickup/delivery verification.
  • Inventory import of delivered items directly into pharmacy stock records.
Workflow
Wholesaler publishes catalog→ Pharmacy sends quote→ Wholesaler responds→ Terms confirmed→ Courier pickup / QR verify→ Inventory import
Commerce
03
Auto Parts Module

A marketplace where customers request parts, vendors send custom offers, and the platform supports part-only or part-plus-service journeys with rider/courier logistics and strong completion controls.

Core Features
  • AI-assisted request drafting converts user problem descriptions into structured part requests.
  • Vendor scoring & matching by location, compatibility, budget, and performance.
  • Custom offer workflow: vendors submit detailed offers (condition, warranty, timeline, price).
  • Offer acceptance engine transitions request/order states and prepares payment/delivery.
  • Delivery quote support: route and courier estimation before dispatch execution.
  • Handover verification using secure code/QR checks.
Integrated Modes
Part-only mode Customer buys part and receives rider/courier delivery.
Part + mechanic mode Accepted part offer can enter mechanic bid/service flow before final completion.
Workflow
Customer raises request→ Vendors send offers→ Customer accepts→ Payment→ Courier dispatch→ Handover verify→ Settlement
Service
04
Mechanic Module

A complete service system supporting direct quote flow, service request statuses, chat/communication, additional-part requests, approval-based payout release, and admin KYC/governance.

Customer & Mechanic Features
  • Direct quote request flow without requiring a vendor-part chain.
  • Quote lifecycle: pending → submitted → accepted/rejected.
  • Service request states: pending, quoted, accepted, in-progress, completed, cancelled, rejected.
  • Additional parts request by mechanic during active service.
  • Chat & notifications for all milestones.
Earnings & Control
  • Escrow-like pending credits held until approval/completion trigger.
  • Approval-gated release on customer approval or job finalization.
  • Distance-based pickup pricing configurable price-per-km.
  • Rating system updates mechanic quality metrics post-service.
Admin Controls
  • KYC decisions with reasons and audit trail.
  • Module stats: active mechanics, job volumes, revenue, rating summaries.
  • Settings control for platform-level mechanic pickup pricing.
Workflow
Quote / offer creation→ Customer acceptance→ Service execution→ Completion request→ Customer approval→ Payout & rating

05
Food Module

Optimized for timing-sensitive fulfillment — combining AI assistance, menu/catalog controls, delayed-dispatch strategy based on prep time, promotions, and POS-ready API integrations.

  • AI food assistant: smart suggestions, meal plan conversations, and guided selection.
  • Preparation-aware dispatch: rider notification waits until food is closer to ready state to reduce idle time and improve freshness.
  • ETA prediction: combines route intelligence and prep estimates for realistic delivery windows.
  • POS integration: vendor APIs and keys for external system synchronization.
  • Promotion & loyalty support: promo validation, usage tracking, and rewards impact at checkout.
  • Multi-order decomposition: split complex orders and coordinate pickup/delivery sequence.
Workflow
Discover items→ Optional AI help→ Checkout with promos→ Prep-aware rider dispatch→ Live tracking→ Completion

Mobility
07
Standalone Riding Module

Powers passenger/courier booking, dispatch waves, rider acceptance, OTP safety checks, and lifecycle-linked earnings.

  • Booking orchestration: creates ride/courier bookings with payment and promo validation.
  • Eligibility-aware request feed: only suitable riders receive each request based on service type and context.
  • Atomic accept flow: request acceptance updates assignment and earning records safely.
  • OTP start verification: trip starts only after secure OTP confirmation at pickup.
  • Live operations: sockets for location, status, and request lifecycle broadcast.

Growth
11
Marketing Management & Automation

A separate growth layer — distinct from promo codes and operational notices — designed for planned campaigns, measurable engagement, and repeatable audience outreach across modules.

Campaigns & Channels
  • Multi-channel outreach: combine push, email, and SMS per campaign strategy.
  • Scheduling & timezones: start/end windows, frequencies (one-off, recurring), and calendar-aware execution.
  • Audience targeting: filter by user types, modules, segments, and geographic/cohort signals.
  • Content & CTAs: titles, body copy, images, deep links, and call-to-action text.
  • A/B testing: variant splits for message optimization.
Segments & Automation
  • Segments: reusable audience groups for campaigns and reporting.
  • Automation rules: trigger-based journeys with execution history and monitoring.
  • Analytics loop: delivery, opens, clicks, conversions, revenue attribution, and health indicators.
Distinction: Promo codes are short redemption mechanics at checkout. Marketing campaigns orchestrate outreach and measurement. System notices are operational broadcasts, not promotions.
Admin
12
System Notices & Operational Broadcasts

Dedicated workflow for communicating maintenance windows, incidents, policy updates, or safety guidance — distinct from marketing campaigns and promotional spam risk.

  • Channels: mobile push (Expo push tokens per user), email, SMS, or in-app inbox-only notices.
  • Lifecycle: draft, schedule for future time, send immediately, or rely on background workers for due schedules.
  • Audience: target by user roles (customers, vendors, riders) with counts from live user data.
  • Scale: delivery runs in controlled batches to avoid overloading a single job.
  • Governance: sent notices are locked; drafts and scheduled items can be edited or removed before dispatch.

Developer, POS & Partner Integrations

Food service and omni-channel vendors often rely on external POS or partner systems. Developer-grade controls keep integrations secure over time.

  • POS & partner APIs: provision credentials for trusted integrations.

  • Optional runtime configuration: Firebase/push and messaging-related settings centralized for consistent mobile behavior.
